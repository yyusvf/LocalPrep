What is this?

My curated collection of Ye and others' edits, put together in playlists by me — featuring noteworthy compilations from the community.

Most of what you see here comes from the Yedits community, a group of fans who take unreleased, unfinished, or poorly mixed Kanye music and finish it themselves.

Everything is playable directly via Samply, a free music app available on iOS, Mac, and as a web app on Windows. All editors are fully credited.
